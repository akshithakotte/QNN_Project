About the Project

Problem Statement

The rise of fake reviews on e-commerce platforms misleads customers and affects trust. Manual detection is time-consuming and prone to error. An automated, intelligent system is required to accurately detect and classify fake reviews.

Solution

We implement a machine learning-based approach using Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques and Quantum Neural Networks (QNN) to classify reviews as genuine or fake. The system analyzes textual patterns, ratings, and user metadata for accurate predictions.

Dataset Features

  • LABEL: Indicates whether the review is genuine (__label1__) or fake (__label2__).

  • PRODUCT_ID: Unique product code (ASIN) for identification.
  • PRODUCT_TITLE: The name or title of the product reviewed.
  • REVIEW_TITLE: The headline provided by the reviewer.
  • REVIEW_TEXT: The detailed text content of the user's review.
